Pay Per Click (PPC)

 

Pay-per-click (PPC), is an internet advertising model used to drive traffic to websites, in which an advertiser pays a publisher when the ad is clicked. Pay-per-click is commonly associated with first-tier search engines (such as Google Ads and Bing Ads).
Webify Services has an expert PPC Team that understands pay-per-click advertising deeply and comes up with unique result-oriented strategies to attract audience to your website and boost conversions. We will update you through periodic performance reports about the success of our PPC marketing campaign.


We follow a systematized PPC process to attract audience to your website and increase conversions:


Strategic Planning
Our PPC experts create a detailed strategy that outlines the core objectives focuses on maximizing clicks, generate leads and promote conversions.

Keyword Research
Based on the objectives of the campaign, relevant keywords are identified and chosen.

Ad Copy Development
Our expert designers develop an eye-catching ad copy that highlights the benefits a potential customer can get by clicking on the link. This generates interest and motivates a user to engage with the business.

Landing Page Optimization
Our knowledge and experience help us to optimize existing landing pages for maximum conversions. This is done by following best landing page development practices.

Monitoring & Report
We continuously monitor a PPC campaign and provide detailed reports from time to time for complete transparency and total control.
