User Roadmap Design

An interaction is a communication between a user and your systems through the interface. Our UX design experts are well-versed in identifying such interactions and creating a roadmap of the system that is consistent with the data flow, users’ expectations and your business goals.

Wireframe Designing

Once User roadmap design are ready, the next step is to visualize how the system, app or a website would look like. This can be done using a prototype known as Wireframes. The wireframes help to ensure that the right information is presented in the right way and at the right place.

Graphics

User Interface (UI) / Look-n-feel of a system is an equally important aspect, along with the well-organized roadmap design. Graphics emphasize on the aesthetics of a system which includes color and contrast, styles, fonts, images, navigation, animation, and many other design elements. A good UI can attract and retain the visitors and increases conversion. At Webify Services we create appealing graphics as per customers’ requirements and branding.

Frontend Development

Frontend development is the process of converting the design into a working system. Webify Services’ frontend development team has expertise in relevant technologies like HTML, JavaScript, CSS. With our years of experience, we ensure that the system works exactly as per the roadmaps.

User Testing

The final stage is the acceptance by the users and we follow User Testing with different users before releasing a system. Based on the valuable user feedbacks, we understand their views and identify scope for improvements.
